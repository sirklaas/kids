# Design Context — Kids YouTube Studio

> A personal creative tool that happens to look like a premium product.

---

## Users

**Primary:** Single creator (myself) using this as a daily creative tool  
**Secondary:** Future collaborators who might review or contribute  

**Context:** Building kids YouTube videos through a 7-stage pipeline — from character concept to video prompts. The tool needs to feel like a creative partner, not a chore.

**Job to be done:** 
- Rapidly prototype and produce video concepts
- Experiment with story variations
- Maintain a professional workflow without corporate stiffness

---

## Brand Personality

**Three words:** Energetic · Refined · Playful

**Voice:** Encouraging but not patronizing. Professional but not stuffy. Creative but not chaotic.

**Emotional goals:**
- Spark creative excitement (⚡ energy)
- Maintain focus during production flow
- Feel premium without being intimidating

**What it IS:**
- A creative studio that happens to be digital
- A playful workspace for serious creation
- Visual delight that supports the work

**What it is NOT:**
- Childish or cartoonish (avoid "kiddy UI")
- Corporate/boring (no Excel vibes)
- Chaotic or overwhelming (energy ≠ noise)

---

## Aesthetic Direction

### Visual Tone
**Minimal with a dash of colour** — clean, spacious layouts with strategic color accents that guide attention and add warmth.

### Depth & Dimension
Embrace the **layered depth design** philosophy:
- Multiple elevation layers with subtle shadows
- Glassmorphism where appropriate (frosted, translucent panels)
- Floating elements that feel tactile
- Smooth, purposeful motion

### Color Palette

**Base:** Dark mode foundation
```
Background:    #09090B (zinc-950) — deep, warm black
Surface:       #18181B (zinc-900) — card backgrounds
Border:        rgba(255,255,255,0.1) — subtle separation
Text Primary:  #FFFFFF — crisp white
Text Secondary: rgba(255,255,255,0.6) — gentle hierarchy
```

**Accent Colors** (use sparingly, one per stage or context):
- **Indigo** (`#6366F1`) — Primary actions, selected states
- **Amber** (`#F59E0B`) — Warnings, highlights, stage indicators  
- **Rose** (`#FB7185`) — Destructive actions, love/heart elements
- **Sky** (`#38BDF8`) — Information, links, secondary accents

**AVOID:** Green (explicitly excluded), neon/bright clashing colors, rainbow gradients

### Typography
- **Primary:** Geist Sans (modern, clean, technical warmth)
- **Monospace:** Geist Mono for data/code displays
- **Scale:** Generous sizing — this is a tool, not dense information display

## Design Principles

### 1. Energy Through Motion
Every interaction should feel alive. Buttons press, cards lift, transitions glide. Animation is not decoration — it's feedback that makes the tool feel responsive and delightful.

### 2. Depth Creates Focus
Use layered elevation to guide the eye. The most important element floats highest. Backgrounds recede, foregrounds invite interaction. Think theater stage lighting.

### 3. Color is Signal
Use the accent palette purposefully. Color means "pay attention here" or "this is active" or "this needs action". Never use color just to "make it pretty".

### 4. Spaciousness is Premium
Generous padding, relaxed line-height, breathing room between elements. A cramped interface feels cheap. A spacious one feels expensive and thoughtful.

### 5. Consistency Over Novelty
Every card looks like every other card. Every button behaves like every other button. Familiarity breeds comfort. Users should never think "where am I now?"

### 6. Dark but Warm
The dark theme should feel cozy, not cold. Avoid pure black (#000000). Use warm darks (zinc-950, not slate-950). Subtle warmth in shadows and highlights.

## Stage Color Coding (Optional Enhancement)

For visual navigation through the 7 stages, consider subtle tinting:

| Stage | Tint | Usage |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1. Characters | Indigo | Primary brand color |
| 2. Story Idea | Amber | Warm, inviting |
| 3. Titles | Rose | Creative spark |
| 4. Synopsis | Violet | Narrative depth |
| 5. Plotboard | Sky | Structural clarity |
| 6. Story | Amber | Back to warmth |
| 7. Video | Indigo | Completion, ready to ship |

**Implementation:** Subtle border-left, stage indicator dot, or active tab underline. Not full theme changes.

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

❌ **Microsoft/Excel vibes:** No sharp corners, no dense grids, no default system blues  
❌ **Kiddy/cartoon UI:** No bouncy animations, no silly icons, no primary colors everywhere  
❌ **Clutter:** No information density for density's sake. One action per view.  
❌ **Flat design:** We use depth, shadows, layers. Flat is boring.  
❌ **Green:** Explicitly excluded from palette (personal preference)
